Champions

Meet the team

Angel

"...Doesn't matter what you try. Doesn't matter where I am or how badass you think you've become. 'Cause you know what? I'm Angel. I beat the bad guys."

Angel moved to Los Angeles believing that even someone as damned as him possessed the potential to become a harbinger of good and redemption, he became a Champion of the Powers That Be and dedicated himself to "helping the helpless" with Angel Investigations in the hope of achieving atonement.

Learn more

Cordelia Chase

"I'm Cordelia. I don't think, I know."

After graduating high school, Cordelia moved to Los Angeles where she joined supernatural detective agency Angel Investigations and gained the power of the visions. These visions gave Cordelia great insight into the pain of others, stripping her of her selfishness and forging her into a dedicated and powerful warrior in the fight against evil.

Learn more

Wesley Wyndam-Pryce

“We shouldn't be keeping score. We're not running a race, we're doing a job. One soul at a time.”

After his dismissal from the Watchers Council, Wesley styled himself as a "rogue demon hunter" and eventually made his way to Los Angeles where he was reunited with Angel and Cordelia. He began assisting their recently opened supernatural detective agency, Angel Investigations, contributing with his educated knowledge of the paranormal and the occult.
